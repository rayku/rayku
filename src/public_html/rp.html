What are RP (Rayku Points)?

Rayku Points, or RP is basically the currency used at Rayku.com.

Currently, one RP equals one Canadian Dollar (1RP = $1CAD).

Ways to Spend RP

  1. Buy anything from the Rayku Shop. We have everything from cash to UofT apparel.
  2. Ask for live expert help (you pay an RP amout per minute of session time).

Ways to Get More RP

  1. Invite your friends to Rayku.com.
  2. Help other people with live expert help (keep Rayku.com open and you will be alerted when someone has a question for you).
  3. Answering questions on the boards (here) in the academic forums, and get selected as 'best answer'.
  4. Become a Rayku-Certified Tutor.
