What is ES (Expert Score)?

Expert score is a metric to measure your activity on Rayku. The higher your expert score, the higher you will rank in search listings.

Once you have reached a certain expert score #, your account will be automatically upgraded to 'Certified', giving you VIP benefits. More info.

Improve your expert score by answering questions by other students, or participating on the boards.
